微软关闭了DreamSpark？

　　昨天晚上收到了微软的邮件，说是关闭了DreamSpark。这个网站从我大一开始就开始用了，验证了学生账户后，从上面下载了很多正版软件，也是我正版意识的出现的契机。

以下是邮件全文：

Hello 康, </p>
<p>As a participant in Microsoft DreamSpark through your school’s WebStore, we’d like to let you know about an update to the program. The DreamSpark name has been retired, but the program benefits continue through Microsoft Imagine. You will still be able to access a variety of free software through your WebStore.</p>
<p>As part of this change, you will experience a new user interface that will make it easy to access your account and keep track of your downloaded software. Please visit your school’s WebStore and accept the new consent language regarding your participation in this program to ensure you can continue to experience all the available benefits and be notified of important updates to the program.<br />
Thanks,<br />
The Microsoft Imagine Team

　　其实可以说是更换了域名，网站模板虽然换了，不过内容大致相同，估计可以得到以前或者更好的体验，也不算是件坏事。
